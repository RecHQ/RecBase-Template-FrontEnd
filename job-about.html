About Us

W12 Design is an enthusiastic and talented creative agency, based in South West London. Our core skills focus on branding, print design and digital, and in particular, the areas where they meet, so we can help you from initial idea to complete brand expression, across all channels.

We blend intelligent creativity with a genuine collaborative approach to produce design that works for our clients. In short: Creativity minus the ego.
