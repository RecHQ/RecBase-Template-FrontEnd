About Us

Since 2012 we’ve helped define, design and prototype digital products for leading media and entertainment companies globally. Our team of over 40 designers, animators and technologists have worked closely with clients to create the next generation of apps, interfaces and experiences.

The Role

We are seeking an inspirational, experienced and engaged Creative Director to join the W12 Studios Leadership Team. You are a visionary and strategic leader that will help us win and execute projects from concept to final delivery. Working directly with clients and multiple creative teams, you will set the bar for quality and innovation to meet or exceed client expectations. Alongside the founders of W12 Studios, you will help foster ongoing improvements to our creative processes and methodologies, feeding these back to the studio. On top of this, you will work with our business development team to introduce and close new business relationships with both new and existing clients.

Responsibilities

Leading, managing and motivating multiple creative teams and projects Working with visual, interaction and motion designers as well as creative technologists to set the strategic vision for the products we design for our clients, and direct them through to realisation Effectively directing and integrating user testing into the iterative design cycles Ensuring a strong line of communication is kept between creative, operation and leadership teams Helping to improve our creative processes and methodologies across the studio Working with ops and production management to ensure that the creative process is efficient and profitable Aiding business development in terms of helping to identify new leads and fostering existing client relationships

Experience & Skills

5+ years digital product design experience - agency or client side - 2+ years of which is experience managing creative teams Visionary, passionate and mature problem solver; adaptable and self motivated History of launching award winning digital products for large, high profile clients Proven track record in people, team and organisational management Strong presentation and communication skills Have excellent attention to detail and proficient knowledge of standard creative software tools e.g. Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch and After Effects Experience working with build teams, delivering designs and assets that can be integrated into production Ability to successfully respond to creative/design RFPs in a consultancy environment Knowledge of best practice design patterns across a range of platforms (iOS, Android, Windows) and devices (mobile, tablet, PC, TV) Deep knowledge of at least one industry sector such as Media and Entertainment Understanding of user experience research methods and processes
